The Problem
Energy waste is invisible —
until the bill arrives

Most households and businesses have no clear picture of where their energy goes. This leads to significant waste, higher costs, and environmental impact.

📊
No Real-Time Visibility
People don't know which appliance consumes how much energy. Without granular data, smart decisions are impossible and energy waste goes unnoticed for months.
💸
Unpredictable Bills
Electricity bills arrive with no explanation. Users have no way to correlate consumption spikes with specific devices or behaviors, making cost reduction feel impossible.
⚠️
No Early Warning System
Overloaded sockets, overheating appliances, and abnormal current draw create fire and safety risks — all invisible without smart monitoring and alerts.
🌍
Growing Environmental Cost
Inefficient energy use contributes to higher carbon emissions. Without actionable insights, even well-meaning users can't make a meaningful environmental impact.
Our Solution
Active management,
not just monitoring

EnergyX is an AI-powered mobile platform that transforms how households and businesses interact with energy. Unlike simple meters or dashboards, EnergyX actively helps users understand, optimize, and save — turning energy efficiency into a daily habit.

EnergyX is built around its own custom IoT hardware — an ESP32-based energy monitoring device with current sensing and safety controls — currently in active prototype development. The platform provides deep analytics, real-time alerts, and personalized AI-driven savings strategies.

✓ No extra hardware needed
✓ Full API integration
✓ AI-personalized insights
